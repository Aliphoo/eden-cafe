Our Story: From Thai Mountains to Your Cup

Eden Cafe was born out of a passion for the art of coffee brewing and supporting Thai farmers. We carefully select coffee beans from the best high-altitude farms in Thailand, roasted with special techniques to achieve a unique flavor. Our minimalist, nature-inspired design offers a true sanctuary for relaxation.

A nature cafe in Nang Lae, Chiang Rai, sharing Thai coffee through highland beans, careful roasting, and a calm space close to nature.

Rooted in Thai coffee

Eden Cafe tells its story through coffee beans grown in Thailand, with attention to quality, freshness, and the character of every cup.

A calm Chiang Rai escape

The cafe is designed as a peaceful place to drink coffee, work, meet friends, or slow down in a nature-inspired Chiang Rai setting.

From Thai mountains to your cup

We focus on Thai highland coffee because cooler growing conditions help develop aroma, natural sweetness, and the layered taste expected from specialty coffee.

About Eden Cafe FAQ

Where is Eden Cafe located?

Eden Cafe is located in Nang Lae, Mueang Chiang Rai, Chiang Rai, Thailand. It is a nature cafe and specialty coffee destination.

What makes Eden Cafe coffee special?

The coffee focuses on Thai highland beans and careful roasting to highlight aroma, balance, and the character of Thai specialty coffee.

Who is Eden Cafe good for?

It is a good fit for visitors looking for a calm Chiang Rai cafe for coffee, work, meeting friends, or relaxing close to nature.
