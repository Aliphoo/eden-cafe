FAQ

Do I need to be a member?

Yes. Please sign in with your existing Eden member account before booking so your booking appears in your member profile.

Can I choose my own lane?

No. The system assigns a lane automatically after your hold is created to avoid overlapping bookings.

How long is the hold?

After you confirm a time, the booking is held for 10 minutes while payment is pending.
